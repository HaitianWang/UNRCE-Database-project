What is ChatGPT?
ChatGPT is a model developed by OpenAI, based on the GPT architecture, designed for generating human-like text.
How does ChatGPT work?
ChatGPT works by taking input text and generating a continuation of that text. It's trained on vast amounts of text data to generate human-like responses.
