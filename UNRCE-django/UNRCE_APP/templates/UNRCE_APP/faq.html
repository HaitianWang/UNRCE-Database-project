Frequently Asked Questions

➤ How can I become involved with the UN RCE WA?
Register your details on this page to join the UN RCE WA network (to receive newsletters and updates). You can also nominate to become a member who attends events and meetings and actively collaborates on education for sustainable development projects.
➤ Does it cost money to join?
Membership of the UN RCE is free. We are all working together toward achieving the UN SDGs and your support and engagement at a local level makes this possible. The bigger the network, the wider the message.
➤ Can I join as an organisation or an individual?
Yes- both. If your organisation is interested in education for sustainable development projects we would love to have you involved. If you are interested in contributing as an individual you are also welcome.
➤ I don’t have a project but I’m interested in becoming involved.
Wonderful! Please contact the secretariat and we will try and connect you with the appropriate working group.
➤ I’m working on an education-based initiative that contributes to the UN SDGs, how can I get involved.
That’s great! Please contact the UN RCE Secretariat to discuss how we can collaborate.
➤ There isn’t a hub in my region but I’m involved in/interested in leading an project focused on education for sustainable development. What can I do?
Currently we have Hubs in Perth and Albany. Please contact the UN RCE Secretariat to discuss how we can support you.

Any other questions please email rce@uwa.edu.au
